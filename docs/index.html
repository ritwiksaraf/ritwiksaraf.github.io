About

I’m Ritwik Saraf — a 4th generation mechanical engineer driven by a deep-rooted passion for engineering innovation and automation. My work spans robotic automation, cleanroom protocols, semiconductor process improvement, and sustainable design. I’m especially fascinated by collaborative robotics and precision systems where design meets code and real-world reliability.

I’m currently pursuing my Master’s in Mechanical Engineering at Purdue University, having previously completed my Bachelor's at Mumbai University. My experience includes working with industry leaders like Applied Materials and research internships with BARC, with key projects in automation, vacuum-based desalination, and smart agriculture systems.

Outside of technical work, I’ve held leadership roles, published in IEEE, and enjoy building systems that bridge the digital-physical gap.
